About

The Story
So Far.

Ibiza, Spain — Currently Based

VLB is a DJ, producer and event organizer from Portugal — now operating out of Ibiza with sights firmly set on the rest of Europe.

The journey started in the underground. 4 years behind the decks, playing over 60 free parties in the first year alone — learning to read crowds before he ever played a stage. That instinct is the foundation of everything.

With 3 years of production, a debut EP, and releases that have earned support from names like I Hate Models and A.N.I — VLB brings a complete artistic identity. Not just a DJ. A full experience.

Currently represented by AMD Agency (FR), with multiple shows in France and a growing presence across Europe.

The Sound

No Fixed
Style.

VLB doesn't do pre-planned sets. Every performance is built in real time — reading the room, tracking energy, and navigating a spectrum from psy to industrial to gabber.

All tracks are rated 1–5 in Rekordbox. The system maps intensity to crowd energy, letting VLB pivot instantly and always deliver the right moment at the right time.

Visual Identity

The
Jetpack.

Handmade. Unmistakable.

Every VLB performance includes a custom-built LED jetpack — designed and assembled entirely by hand. Illuminated tubes, a glowing VLB sign, and programmable light effects that evolve with the energy of the set.

This isn't just an outfit. It's the visual signature of a complete audiovisual concept — a connection between sound, light, artist, and crowd that makes every performance a moment people carry home.

"The goal isn't just to play music. It's to create a moment — unified, present, and alive."

The Label

Estrago
Label.

Founded by VLB, Estrago is Portugal's underground rave collective — built from nothing, by hand. From lineup to lighting, sound system to visual identity: every detail is handled in-house.

Events regularly sell out at 1,200+ capacity. The crowd, the chaos, the energy — all of it curated from the ground up.

Estrago isn't just a label. It's proof that VLB doesn't just know how to perform — he knows how to build an entire world around it.

Career Journey

How We
Got Here.

4 Years Ago
Bedroom DJ & Free Parties
Started on a pair of decks in a bedroom — then hit the underground fast. Over 60 shows in the first year alone, playing free parties across Portugal and learning to read a crowd before ever playing a stage.
3 Years Ago
First Events & Estrago Founded
Started organizing events to create stages for himself and others. Founded Estrago — handling everything from sound and lighting to booking and promotion.
2–3 Years Ago
Production · Studio · Content
Entered the studio. Started producing original music. Built a SoundCloud presence and began documenting the journey through digital content — turning every gig into a story.
1–2 Years Ago
National Recognition · Estrago Scales
Playing venues across all of Portugal. Estrago events hitting 1,200+ capacity sell-outs. The Rekordbox system refined. The jetpack built. The brand taking shape.
1 Year Ago
AMD Agency · France · Industry Support
Signed with AMD Agency (FR). Multiple shows in France including the Insane Festival Tour. Tracks supported by I Hate Models, A.N.I, Lorenzo Raganzini and more.
Now — Ibiza
Expanding. Spain. Europe. The World.
Based in Ibiza. Actively seeking bookings across Spain and Europe. New music in progress. The next chapter is being written right now.
